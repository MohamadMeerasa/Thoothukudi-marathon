2.What about the weather in Thoothukudi on the race weekend?

The weather in Thoothukudi is generally sunny and Humid, with temperatures of 25 to 32 degrees C. However, Runners are requested to dress appropriately and ensure they are sufficiently hydrated throughout the course.

3. Will the event be called off incase of heavy rain?

Will the event be called off in the event of heavy rain? Calling off the event due to unfavourable weather is at the discretion of the organisers. We believe that the well-being of the runners is a priority, and any weather conditions that necessitate the race being called off will be thoroughly deliberated by the organisers before a decision is taken. There will be no refund of the registration fee in case the race is called off due to weather or any other acts of God (force majeure clauses), and the decision of the Race Director is final in this regard.

5. Can I use IPOD / Head Phones during the course of the race?

Can I use IPOD / Head Phones during the course of the race? Yes!! We believe music is a great aid to running. However, please be alert to route signage, announcements from the race marshals, and traffic (in case you exceed event cut-off times).

6. Will there be AID Stations and emergency medical assistance available along the route?

Will there be AID stations and emergency medical assistance available along the route? Of course! We have Aid Stations stocked with water, energy drinks, biscuits, and bananas along the route. In addition to ambulances, first-aid stations and medical outposts are also present along the route. Please refer to the route maps in the respective categories.

7. Is there a place to store my baggage on Race day?

Is there a place to store my baggage on race day? If you are carrying any baggage on race day, it can be kept at the venue because we will be finishing in the same place where we are starting. All baggage will be screened for security reasons, and the organisers of the marathon are not responsible for any losses or damage to the contents. We recommend not having anything valuable or fragile in your bag.

8. How do I get back to start point after finishing the race?

How do I get back to the start point after finishing the race? People unable to finish the race completely should make sure to wait at the water stations or medical aid stations. Vehicles will be arranged to pick you up and bring you to the finish line venue.

9. What about parking at the start point?

There are parking lots available at the start point, at the Tharavai Ground. These have limited spaces and can be occupied on a "first come, first served basis". We strongly recommend car pooling.

Note: Parking is at your own risk, and the organisers of the marathon are not liable for the same.
